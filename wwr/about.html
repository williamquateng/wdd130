History

The early history of white water rafting can be linked to LieutenantJohn Fremont and his exploration of the Rocky Mountains in the 1840s. Supported by Horace H. Day, Fremont embarked on a daring expedition, utilizing rubber rafts to navigate the treacherous waters of the Great Plains.
